Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to the questions we hear most often.

How do I reset my password?

Go to the Login page and click "Forgot password?" — we will email you a reset link within 2 minutes.

Can I change my plan after signing up?

Yes. Head to your Dashboard → Settings → Billing and upgrade or downgrade at any time.

My PDF looks different from the preview.

Make sure you are using the latest Chrome or Edge browser. Safari may render fonts slightly differently.

Do you offer refunds?

We offer a full refund within 7 days of any paid subscription — no questions asked.

Is my data stored securely?

All data is encrypted in transit and at rest. We never sell or share your personal information.

Can I use ResumeForge on mobile?

Yes — the site is fully responsive. For the best building experience we recommend a desktop browser.
